Why are we

Holding this conference?

In addition to being a solid framework in constant evolution accompanied by an important number of tools that complement its operation, what makes Angular great worldwide is its community: Periodically around the world a lot of developers meet in local meetups to share and update their knowledge on this technology.

In addition, more and more countries around the world hold an annual conference that brings together a larger number of international assistants and speakers to offer an experience of great complexity and impact.

In our region of Latin America, Colombia pioneered the 2017 and 2018 versions of Ng Colombia in which more than 500 attendees, 20 speakers from all over the world and 6 workshops can be counted. Since its creation, this conference has been characterized by taking care of all the details and providing an unforgettable experience.

This year, under the name of NgConf Colombia, the conference is organized by a group of leaders from local communities in Angular de Bogotá and Medellín and expects to receive more than 400 attendees and 12 speakers.
