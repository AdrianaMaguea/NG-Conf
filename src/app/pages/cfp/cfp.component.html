We are pleased to announce that the largest Angular conference in Spanish-speaking opened its call for Speakers. Starting today and until next May 31, we will be receiving all the proposals for conducting talks and workshops on all topics related to Angular and front end development technologies.

No matter the country you come from, your experience or the language you speak, if you want to participate, we welcome you. We want to inspire as many people around the world as possible and that is why your participation and the energy you can impress on it is so important to us. Available Categories

NgConfCo 2020 will have 3 different categories for the realization of each intervention:

Full Talks

Talks in a 20-minute format. We will discuss technical topics related to the Angular platform.

Lightning talks

Talks in 10 minute format. We will deal with technical topics related to web development, as well as relevant non-technical topics in our work as developers.

WORKSHOPS

Live master classes with duration of 4. The topics are totally focused on the Angular platform, and are aimed at a mixed audience with basic to intermediate knowledge.
