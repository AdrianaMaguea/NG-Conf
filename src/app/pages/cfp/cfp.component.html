Available Categories

NgConfCo 2020 will have 3 different categories for the realization of each intervention:

Full Talks

If you consider the topic you are going to talk is big enough to be detailed explained, this 20-minutes intervention is for you.

Lightning talks

The 10 minutes format allows a wide variety of people to participate as speakers and articulate a topic in a quick, insightful, and clear manner.

WORKSHOPS

Live master classes with duration of 4 hours. The topics are totally focused on the Angular platform, and are aimed at a mixed audience with basic to intermediate knowledge.
